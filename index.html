Ganesh Chaturthi will be celebrated on September 7, 2024, a Saturday.
The festival pays homage to Lord Ganesh, the remover of obstacles, and celebrates the day of his birth.
At the start of the holiday, Ganesha statues are put up in homes, temples, and public places.
People who worship Lord Ganesha say prayers, sing devotional songs and do rituals to welcome the god's arrival.
